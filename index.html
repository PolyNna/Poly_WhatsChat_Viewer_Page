How to Use App

Follow these simple steps to browse your WhatsApp chats:

  1. Open the App: Launch Poly WhatsChat Viewer on your Android device.
  2. Load Chat Backup: Select your exported WhatsApp chat file (txt or zip format).
  3. Browse Messages: Messages appear in interactive color-coded bubbles for easy reading.
  4. Preview Media: Tap images, videos, or voice notes to view or play directly.
  5. Search Chats: Use the search bar to find specific messages, links, or media filenames.
  6. Adjust Settings: Switch themes, set wallpapers, and tweak brightness for comfort.
  7. Navigate Efficiently: Jump to first or last message, or view chat statistics.
  8. Clear Chat View: Temporarily clear messages without deleting the original backup.
